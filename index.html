Elexoft ("we," "our," or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Smart Expense Tracker collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our mobile application. By using Smart Expense Tracker, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
01

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your full name and email address. Authentication credentials are managed through:

  • Email and password
  • Google Sign-In
  • Sign in with Apple

Authentication is securely managed using Supabase Authentication.

Financial Information

To provide expense tracking features, we store information that you choose to enter, including:

  • Expense records
  • Income records
  • Categories
  • Budgets
  • Bills
  • Notes
  • Receipt images

This information is stored securely in our cloud database powered by Supabase.

Voice Input

If you choose to use voice input, the application accesses your device's microphone to capture your spoken expense or income details. The recorded voice is processed to convert speech into text and generate a structured entry using AI-powered processing.

🎙

This processing may involve securely transmitting the audio or transcribed content to OpenAI for the purpose of interpreting and organizing the information you provide. Voice input is only used when you explicitly initiate this feature.

Receipt Scanning

If you choose to scan a receipt, you may:

  • Capture a receipt using your device's camera
  • Select an existing receipt image from your photo library

Receipt images are processed using AI-powered services to extract expense information and generate a structured record for your review before it is saved.

02

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Authenticate your identity
  • Synchronize your financial data across your devices
  • Store and organize expenses, income, budgets, bills, categories, notes, and receipts
  • Convert voice input into structured financial records
  • Extract expense information from receipt images
  • Improve the reliability and functionality of the application
  • Respond to customer support requests
03

Permissions We Request

Camera

Used to capture receipt images for expense recording.

Microphone

Used only when you choose voice input to record spoken expense or income information.

Speech Recognition

Used to convert your speech into text for creating financial records.

Photo Library / Storage

Used to allow you to select receipt images stored on your device.

Internet Access

Required to authenticate users, synchronize data with Supabase, and process AI-powered voice and receipt recognition.

Biometric Authentication

If enabled by you, Face ID or Fingerprint can be used to unlock the app securely.

🔒

Your biometric information is never collected, stored, or transmitted by Elexoft. Authentication is performed entirely by your device's operating system.

04

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to provide essential functionality.

Supabase
Provides user authentication, secure cloud database, and data storage. Your account and financial records are stored securely using Supabase infrastructure.
OpenAI
When you use voice input or receipt scanning, your audio, transcribed text, or receipt image may be securely processed by OpenAI solely to generate structured expense information. We do not use this information to advertise to you.
Google Sign-In
If you choose Google Sign-In, Google securely authenticates your identity and provides your basic account information, such as your name and email address.
Sign in with Apple
If you choose Sign in with Apple, Apple securely authenticates your identity and may provide your name and email address, depending on your Apple account settings.

If you delete your account, your personal information and associated financial records will be removed in accordance with our data deletion processes, except where retention is required by applicable law.
